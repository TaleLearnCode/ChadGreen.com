## Other topics to include in social media postings

1. Session preview posts (problem statement, audience takeaway, who should attend).
2. Travel and arrival updates (especially day-before conference presence).
3. Speaker reception and behind-the-scenes moments.
4. Day-of reminders for session attendance.
5. Post-session thank-you posts to attendees.
6. Key takeaway recap posts from each talk.
7. Cross-promotion of the next engagement (chronologically) for the next event.
8. Meetup/community call-to-action posts (local audience focus).
9. Image-first posts using title slides, room photos, and recap galleries.
10. Sponsor/community appreciation shout-outs when relevant.
