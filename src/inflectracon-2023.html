Event Overview

InflectraCON™ 2023 is a three-day multi-track conference celebrating all things Software Development, Testing, Agile, DevOps, Cyber, Leadership, and Innovation. Set in the heart of the nation’s capital, InflectraCON 2023 will have multiple conference tracks Quality, Agility, and Security. Be part of the most exhilarating conversations about digital transformation.

The conference will also host Inflectra’s Partner Summit and User Tutorials featuring user training, deep-dives, and certification on our award-winning platforms: Spira* and Rapise.

The event will be hosted in person in Washington, DC, on April 19-21, 2023!
