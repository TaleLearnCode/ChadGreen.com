## Navigating the Maze of Communicating Architecture

Communicating architecture is hard because the context, tradeoffs, and decisions are often scattered. This session focuses on practical ways to make architecture communication clearer and more durable.

### What this session covers

- Turning conversations into durable decision records.
- Capturing context, tradeoffs, and consequences with less ambiguity.
- Helping teams reuse architecture decisions instead of rediscovering them.
