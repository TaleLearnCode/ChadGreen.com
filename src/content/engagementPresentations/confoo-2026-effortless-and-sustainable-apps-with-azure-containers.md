Thanks for joining this session at **ConFoo 2026** in Montreal.

## Effortless & Sustainable Apps with Azure Containers

Discover how Azure Container Apps simplify containerization by delivering enterprise-grade capabilities without Kubernetes complexity. Learn how built-in scaling, integrated services, and strong security enable effortless, sustainable deployments. Through real-world insights and best practices, explore ways to optimize resources, cut costs, and streamline orchestration for long-term success.

### What This Session Covers

This talk focuses on practical ways to adopt containers in Azure without taking on unnecessary operational overhead.

You’ll see how Azure container services can help teams ship faster while staying reliable, secure, and cost-aware through:

- built-in scaling patterns
- integrated platform capabilities
- secure-by-default deployment practices
- streamlined orchestration choices that avoid premature complexity

### Practical Outcomes

By the end of the session, you should be able to:

- Identify when managed container platforms are a better fit than full Kubernetes management.
- Apply scaling and resource optimization approaches that support long-term sustainability.
- Reduce operational friction for teams that are modernizing existing applications.
