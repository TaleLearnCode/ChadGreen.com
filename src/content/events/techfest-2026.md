TechFest Louisville 2026 is the 8th annual edition of Louisville’s premier technology event, hosted by TALK — the Technology Association of Louisville, Kentucky. Taking place August 20–21, 2026 at Savor at River House, the two‑day conference brings together more than 285 professionals across business, government, startups, higher education, and the regional tech community. 

The event focuses on the rapidly evolving impact of AI, Cybersecurity, Healthcare IT, Natural Language Processing (NLP), and Robotics/Automation. Attendees gain access to case studies, in‑depth learning sessions, how‑to guidance, and real‑world implementation stories that highlight how organizations across Kentucky and the region are transforming through modern technology. 

TechFest Louisville emphasizes practical, enterprise‑ready insights—covering topics such as AI governance, multi‑tiered technology solutions, robotics in manufacturing, AI‑powered healthcare workflows, and data engineering for enterprise transformation. The conference also features a full trade‑show experience and a lineup of regional innovators presenting on emerging trends like virtual reality for wellness, AI‑driven surgical status verification, and agent‑based system design. 

As Louisville’s employer‑led, non‑profit tech council, TALK curates TechFest to support the region’s growing technology ecosystem, offering accessible professional development, networking opportunities, and exposure to cutting‑edge tools shaping the future of work.
