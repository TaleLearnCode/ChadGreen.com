## About THAT Conference

THAT Conference is a geek-focused community conference held at Kalahari Resorts in Wisconsin Dells. Known for its welcoming, inclusive atmosphere and coverage of software development, hardware, DevOps, and the maker mindset. The 2021 edition ran as a hybrid in-person and online event.

## Event Details

**Dates:** July 26–29, 2021  
**Location:** Kalahari Resorts & Conventions, Wisconsin Dells, WI (Hybrid)
