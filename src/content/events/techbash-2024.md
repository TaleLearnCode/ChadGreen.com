## About TechBash

TechBash is an annual developer conference held at the Kalahari Resorts in the Pocono Mountains of Pennsylvania. The conference brings together software developers, architects, and technology professionals for several days of learning, networking, and hands-on workshops.

Known for combining high-quality technical content with a family-friendly resort setting, TechBash features a wide range of sessions covering cloud architecture, modern development practices, DevOps, and emerging technologies.

## Event Details

**Dates:** September 24–27, 2024  
**Location:** Kalahari Resorts & Conventions, Pocono Manor, PA
