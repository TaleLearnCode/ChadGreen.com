## About CodeMash

CodeMash is one of the largest community-driven technology conferences in North America. Held annually at the Kalahari Resorts in Sandusky, Ohio, CodeMash brings together software developers, architects, and technology professionals for two days of learning, networking, and professional development.

The conference features a diverse range of sessions covering topics from cloud architecture and microservices to security, AI, and software craftsmanship. Whether you're a seasoned architect or an early-career developer, CodeMash offers valuable insights and networking opportunities.

## Event Details

**Dates:** January 16-17, 2025  
**Location:** [Kalahari Resorts & Conventions](https://www.kalahariresorts.com/sandusky), Sandusky, OH

## Community Driven

CodeMash is built on community participation, with speakers ranging from local professionals to industry experts. The conference emphasizes practical, real-world knowledge sharing and creates an environment where attendees can learn from their peers, make connections, and grow professionally.
