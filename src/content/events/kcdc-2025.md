## About KCDC

KCDC (Kansas City Developer Conference) is one of the premier developer conferences in the Midwest, held annually at the Kansas City Convention Center. The conference draws thousands of attendees from across the region and beyond for multi-day programming covering cloud architecture, software development, security, DevOps, and emerging technologies.

KCDC is known for its high-quality speaker lineup, welcoming community atmosphere, and broad range of sessions suitable for developers at all experience levels.
