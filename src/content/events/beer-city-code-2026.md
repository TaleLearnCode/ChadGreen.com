Beer City Code is a vibrant, community‑driven software development conference held annually in Grand Rapids, Michigan, bringing together developers, engineers, architects, and technology enthusiasts for two days of learning, collaboration, and fun. Hosted in the heart of “Beer City USA,” the event blends high‑quality technical content with the welcoming, craft‑beer‑infused culture the city is known for.

The conference features a full day of hands‑on workshops followed by a multi‑track conference day, covering topics across .NET, JavaScript, cloud, DevOps, AI, security, architecture, testing, and modern development practices. Sessions are led by industry experts and community leaders, offering practical guidance, real‑world insights, and opportunities to sharpen both technical and professional skills.

Beer City Code is known for its friendly atmosphere, strong regional community, and commitment to accessible, high‑value learning. Attendees enjoy not only the educational experience but also the social events, networking opportunities, and the chance to explore Grand Rapids’ nationally recognized craft beer scene.

Whether you're a seasoned engineer or early in your career, Beer City Code provides an engaging, inclusive environment to grow your skills, connect with peers, and celebrate the craft of software development.
