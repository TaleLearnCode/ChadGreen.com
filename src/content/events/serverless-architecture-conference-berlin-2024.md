## About Serverless Architecture Conference Berlin

The Serverless Architecture Conference is Europe's premier event dedicated to serverless computing, cloud-native architectures, and event-driven development. Held in Berlin, the conference brings together developers, architects, and cloud practitioners for deep-dive sessions on serverless design patterns, cloud platforms, and best practices.

## Event Details

**Dates:** October 21–24, 2024  
**Location:** Hotel Palace Berlin, Berlin, Germany
