## About KCDC

KCDC (Kansas City Developer Conference) is one of the premier developer conferences in the Midwest, held annually at the Kansas City Convention Center. The conference draws thousands of attendees for multi-day programming covering cloud architecture, software development, security, DevOps, and emerging technologies.
