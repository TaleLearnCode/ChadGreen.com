## About SDD 2026

The Software Design & Development (SDD) Conference is the main annual event for software developers and architects, featuring 92 sessions and workshops that offer unparalleled depth and breadth in software development education, delivered by world-class speakers who are acknowledged experts in their fields and have years of experience educating developers.

Key topics include

- **Generative AI & Machine Learning:** How LLMs work, Semantic AI, and practical AI applications
- **Software Architecture:** Architecture trade-offs, design patterns, and architectural decision-making  
- **Modern Development:** Microservices, .NET, C#, and cloud-native patterns
- **Software Design:** Design patterns, software reliability, and maintainable code
- **Hands-On Workshops:** Full-day intensive workshops on cutting-edge topics
