## About THAT Conference

THAT Conference is a geek-focused community conference held at Kalahari Resorts in Wisconsin Dells. Known for its welcoming, inclusive atmosphere and "open spaces" format alongside traditional sessions, THAT covers software development, hardware, DevOps, and the maker mindset—all in a family-friendly resort environment.

## Event Details

**Dates:** July 25–28, 2022  
**Location:** Kalahari Resorts & Conventions, Wisconsin Dells, WI
