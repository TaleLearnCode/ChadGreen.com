## About Beer City Code

Beer City Code is a community-driven developer conference held in Grand Rapids, Michigan, known for its welcoming atmosphere and commitment to fostering local talent and community involvement. The conference brings together software developers, architects, and technology professionals for a day of learning, networking, and professional development.

With sessions covering modern development practices, cloud architecture, software design, and emerging technologies, Beer City Code offers valuable insights for developers at all levels of experience. The conference is known for its emphasis on community engagement and supporting local speakers.

## Event Details

**Date:** August 2-3, 2024  
**Location:** Calvin University, Fine Arts Center, Grand Rapids, MI

## Community Celebration

Beer City Code celebrates the vibrant developer community in Grand Rapids and the Midwest. By bringing together local and regional speakers, the conference creates opportunities for knowledge sharing, networking, and community building within the software development profession.
