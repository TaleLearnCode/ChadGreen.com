## About dev up 2025

dev up is a premier developer conference serving the Midwest software development community. With a focus on cutting-edge technologies, software architecture, and professional development, dev up brings together developers, architects, and technology leaders for intensive learning and networking.

The conference features expert speakers sharing practical insights on cloud architecture, microservices, security, and emerging technologies. With sessions ranging from beginner to advanced levels, dev up caters to developers at all stages of their careers.
