## About Michigan Technology Conference

The Michigan Technology Conference is a gathering of technology professionals in Michigan dedicated to advancing knowledge and skills in software development, cloud architecture, and technology innovation. The conference features expert speakers sharing practical insights and best practices on the latest technologies and development trends.

With a focus on professional development and community building, the Michigan Technology Conference provides valuable networking opportunities and educational sessions for developers and technology leaders across the state.

## Event Details

**Date:** March 21, 2024  
**Location:** [The Towne Club](https://www.thetowneclub.com/), Pontiac, MI

## Professional Development

The conference is committed to fostering professional growth within Michigan's technology community through knowledge sharing, networking, and collaborative learning experiences.
