Visual Studio Live! Orlando 2026 is part of Live! 360 Tech Con at the Loews Royal Pacific Resort in Orlando, FL. Co-located with TechMentor, Artificial Intelligence Live, AI Enterprise Architecture Live, Cybersecurity & Ransomware Live, and Data Platform Live, the event runs November 15–20 with breakout sessions November 17–19.

VSLive! brings together .NET and Microsoft ecosystem professionals for immersive, practitioner-led training covering Visual Studio, .NET, ASP.NET Core, Generative AI, GitHub Copilot, Azure, Blazor, C#, and modern data solutions.

## Conference Highlights

Sessions are delivered by industry experts and Microsoft insiders, going beyond theory to provide real-world insights and practical guidance across native client development, web, cloud, AI, and data technologies.

### Track Topics

- **Modern Software Engineering:** DevOps, GitHub, testing, and agile practices
- **Cloud, Containers & Microservices:** Azure, serverless, container orchestration
- **Cutting-Edge AI:** Generative AI, machine learning, Azure AI services
- **Data & Analytics:** SQL Server, Azure SQL, Entity Framework, data modernization
- **Developing New Experiences:** MAUI, Blazor, web development
- **Human Factors in Engineering:** Team dynamics, communication, career development
- **The Core of .NET:** C#, .NET, performance optimization
- **Full Stack Web Development:** ASP.NET Core, Blazor, modern web patterns

### Co-Located Conferences

Live! 360 Tech Con brings together multiple premier conferences under one roof:

- **TechMentor** — IT professional training
- **Artificial Intelligence Live** — AI strategy and implementation
- **AI Enterprise Architecture Live** — Enterprise-scale AI architecture
- **Cybersecurity & Ransomware Live** — Security and threat defense
- **Data Platform Live** — Data engineering and analytics

## Venue & Location

The Loews Royal Pacific Resort is a tropical-themed resort at Universal Orlando Resort, offering world-class conference facilities, multiple dining options, and easy access to Universal's theme parks and Orlando's entertainment district.
