The Kansas City Developers Conference (KCDC) 2026 is the 18th annual edition of one of the Midwest’s largest and most respected software development conferences. Held September 9–11, 2026 at the Kansas City Convention Center in downtown Kansas City, the event brings together a broad community of developers, architects, UI/UX designers, QA professionals, project managers, and technology leaders from across Missouri, Kansas, Illinois, Nebraska, Iowa, Minnesota, Oklahoma, the Dakotas, and beyond. 

KCDC features a pre-conference workshop day on September 9, followed by two full conference days on September 10–11, offering deep technical sessions, hands-on learning, and opportunities to connect with industry experts. 

The conference is known for its wide-ranging content, covering modern software development practices, cloud technologies, architecture, leadership, DevOps, frontend and backend frameworks, testing, and emerging trends across the tech ecosystem. Its welcoming, community-driven environment attracts both new and experienced professionals seeking practical skills, career growth, and meaningful networking.

KCDC also hosts Kids Tech Day on September 12, extending its commitment to education and community impact by introducing younger learners to technology in a fun, hands-on format. 

Whether you're attending workshops, exploring sessions across multiple tracks, or connecting with peers and speakers, KCDC 2026 offers a vibrant, inclusive experience designed to help technologists learn, grow, and stay ahead in a rapidly evolving industry.
