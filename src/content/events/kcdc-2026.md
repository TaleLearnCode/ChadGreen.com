## About KCDC

The Kansas City Developer Conference is excited to announce our 18th annual event! The 2026 conference will be held September 9th-11th, with a pre-conference workshop day and two full conference days. Once again, the event will be held at the Kansas City Convention Center in downtown Kansas City. Each year, we draw a large audience of new and experienced Developers, Architects, UI/UX Designers, QA Professionals, PMs, and Technology Managers from Missouri, Kansas, Illinois, Nebraska, Iowa, Minnesota, Oklahoma, the Dakotas, and around the United States and the world. We hope you will join us!

**Dates:** September 9-11, 2026
