## About Nebraska.Code()

Nebraska.Code() is a regional developer conference serving the Midwest software development community. Held in Lincoln, Nebraska, the conference brings together developers, architects, and technology professionals for a multi-day program of sessions, workshops, and networking.

The conference covers a broad range of topics including cloud architecture, modern development practices, DevOps, and software design, with a strong emphasis on community participation and professional growth.

## Event Details

**Dates:** July 17–19, 2024  
**Location:** Lincoln Marriott Cornhusker Hotel, Lincoln, NE
