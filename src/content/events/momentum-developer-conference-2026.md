The Momentum Developer Conference (Momentum DevCon) is Cincinnati’s flagship event for software developers, bringing together professionals from across the region and around the world to sharpen skills, make connections, and geek out with impunity. Hosted at the Hard Rock Casino Cincinnati on October 16, 2026, the conference delivers a full day of high‑value technical content, community engagement, and hands‑on learning.

Attendees gain access to 35 sessions of outstanding content presented by speakers from Cincinnati, the Midwest, and global tech communities. Topics span modern software development practices, emerging technologies, architecture, cloud, DevOps, AI, and professional growth. The event emphasizes practical, real‑world insights developers can apply immediately.

Momentum is known for its energetic, community‑driven atmosphere. Beyond the sessions, attendees are encouraged to explore Cincinnati’s vibrant tech and social scene—visiting the Banks, touring microbreweries, or even throwing axes with friends—making the conference both professionally enriching and genuinely fun.

With breakfast, lunch, and refreshments included, plus ample opportunities to connect with fellow developers, Momentum DevCon serves as a career catapult for technologists looking to grow, network, and stay ahead in a rapidly evolving industry.
