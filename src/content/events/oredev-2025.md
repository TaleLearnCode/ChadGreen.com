## About Øredev 2025

Øredev is one of Europe's premier developer conferences, held annually in Malmö, Sweden. The conference brings together developers, architects, and technology leaders from across the globe for sessions covering software craftsmanship, architecture, cloud, and the latest in modern development practices.
