## About TechBash 2019

TechBash is a developer conference held at Kalahari Resort in Pocono Manor, Pennsylvania. The conference combines technical learning with family-friendly activities, bringing together developers and technology professionals for sessions on cloud, software development, DevOps, and emerging technologies.

**Dates:** November 13–15, 2019
