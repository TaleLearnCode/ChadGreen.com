Immerse yourself in a comprehensive full-day workshop that delves into the transformative world of serverless computing using Azure and .NET. This workshop is designed for developers and architects eager to leverage serverless technologies to construct scalable, efficient, and cost-effective solutions.

Throughout this intensive day, you will participate in practical activities illuminating the fundamental concepts, tools, and best practices for architecting serverless applications on the Azure platform using the .NET framework.

This workshop focuses on building a real-world business scenario for Cool Revive Technologies—a company that specializes in breathing new life into old refrigerators. By refurbishing and optimizing these appliances, they contribute to sustainability, reduce waste, and provide affordable solutions for consumers. You will build a solution for Cool Revive's remanufacturing process through hands-on labs, experiencing first-hand how serverless technologies can modernize and automate complex business workflows.

By the end of the workshop, you will have acquired hands-on experience designing, developing, and deploying serverless solutions on Azure using .NET, equipping you with the skills to effectively incorporate serverless technologies into your own projects.

### Target Audience

- Developers and architects eager to build scalable, cost-effective serverless solutions
- Teams exploring Azure Functions and serverless-first design
- Anyone looking for hands-on experience with Azure's event-driven and serverless services

### Prerequisites

- Working knowledge of C# and .NET development
- Basic familiarity with cloud concepts (an Azure account is required for labs)
- Understanding of REST APIs and basic software design principles
- Laptop with Visual Studio or VS Code, .NET SDK, and Azure CLI installed
